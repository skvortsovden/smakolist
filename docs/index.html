Smakolist is a free app for tracking your daily meals. Add your own recipes, log what you eat, and review your habits over time. All data is stored only on your device — no account needed, no data collected, no internet required.

What you can do:

  • Recipes — add and manage your own dishes
  • Today — log meals for breakfast, lunch, dinner and snacks
  • Calendar — review and edit entries for past days
  • Lists — build shopping lists from recipes or manually
  • Reminders — get notified at meal times

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

Data Collection

Smakolist does not collect, store, or share any personal data with third parties.

Data Storage

All data (recipes, meal logs, shopping lists, settings) is stored exclusively on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers.

Notifications

If you enable meal reminders, they are scheduled locally by iOS. No information is transmitted to any external service.
